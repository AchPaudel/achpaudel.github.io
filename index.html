About me

I'm a dreamer. I'm an imaginer. I'm a life-long learner.

From early on, this world fascinated me. Luckily for me, in this age of technology, the internet offers us a collection of seemingly unlimited knowledge. It's all just one click away.
It is through reading, watching, and asking questions that I fell in love with science. Similar to art, I saw within it, an inexplicable essence of beauty.

Accordingly, my interest in coding and development arose as a result of this never-ending curiosity and imagination.
Since a child, I have dreamt of a future with flying cars. But, I also dreamt of contributing and being involved in such a pivotal invention. Jetpacks? I want to help code the stabilization system. Holographic phones? I have some good ideas that could help.
I want to fulfill that childhood-bred curiosity.
